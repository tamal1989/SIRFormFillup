Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 09, 2025

SIR West Bengal Helper ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website to prepare the SIR Annexure-III form.

1. We Do Not Store Your Personal Data

We do not store any personal information you enter in the SIR form — including name, Aadhaar number, EPIC number, address, photo, or signature. All form data is processed in your browser and sent directly to Formspree for email delivery. We have no access to your submitted data.

2. Data Sent via Formspree

When you submit the form, your data is sent securely to Formspree (a third-party service). Formspree:

  • Emails you the filled PDF
  • Does not store your data permanently
  • Deletes submission after 30 days
We do not receive or store the email or PDF. Formspree Privacy Policy

3. Google Analytics (Anonymized)

We use Google Analytics to count page views and improve user experience. It collects:

  • Anonymous visit data (no names, no IP full tracking)
  • Page views, time on site, device type
No personal identification is possible. IP anonymization is enabled. Google Privacy Policy

4. Google AdSense

We display non-personalized ads via Google AdSense. Ads may set cookies to show relevant content. We do not control ad content. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for form functionality (e.g., remembering input temporarily). No tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser — the form will still work.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site links to CEO West Bengal, ECI, and PDF downloads. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties except Formspree (for email delivery) and Google (analytics & ads).

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption. Since we don’t store data, breach risk is zero.

10. Your Rights

Since we don’t store data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Contact Formspree if you want submission logs removed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.

12. Contact Us

Email: privacy@sirwestbengal.com
