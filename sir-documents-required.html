SIR Documents Required & Photo Size – West Bengal 2025

Complete list of mandatory and optional documents. Photo specifications. Avoid BLO rejection.

Mandatory Documents (Must Bring)
  • Aadhaar Card – Original + 1 self-attested photocopy
  • Recent Color Photo – 4 cm × 3 cm (passport size)
  • Current Voter ID (EPIC) – If you have one

Note: Aadhaar is the primary identity proof. Without it, verification fails.

Optional Documents (Bring if Available)
  • 2002 Voter List Proof – From our list
  • Address Proof – Electricity bill, rent agreement, bank passbook
  • Birth Certificate – If DOB mismatch in Aadhaar
  • School Leaving Certificate – For age proof
  • Ration Card – With photo
Photo Size & Rules (Strict)

Size: 4 cm (height) × 3 cm (width)
Background: White only
Face: Full front, eyes open, no glasses, no cap
Format: Color, recent (within 6 months)
Action: Paste on printed form (do not staple)

Wrong photo = Rejection
