How to Fill SIR Form 2025 West Bengal – Step by Step Guide

Avoid BLO rejection. Fill Annexure-III form correctly in 10 minutes. Free sample, common mistakes, and tips included.

Step 1: Gather Required Documents Before Filling

Collect these items: Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy), 2002 voter list proof (if available), current Voter ID, and address proof (electricity bill, bank passbook). Without 2002 proof, don’t worry — BLO will verify via neighbors. Keep everything ready to avoid last-minute panic.

Step 2: Fill Top Section – Personal Details

Write your full name exactly as in Aadhaar in the first box. Enter Date of Birth in DD/MM/YYYY format. In family EPIC section, fill Father’s, Mother’s, or Spouse’s Voter ID number only if known. Use ‘Not Known’ if unsure. This helps BLO trace family linkage.

Step 3: Copy Electoral Roll Details (Left Column)

Get your current voter list PDF from your BLO or download from our 2002 list checker. Copy your name, EPIC, father’s name, AC number, Part No., and Serial No. exactly as printed — even if wrong. This is your baseline record.

Step 4: Fill SIR Column (Right Side) – Current Status

Write your correct name, address, and status here. If you shifted, mention new AC. If name changed after marriage, write new name. If alive and same address, repeat left column data. This column decides your final voter list entry.

Step 5: Tick All 3 Declaration Boxes

Read carefully and tick all three:

  1. You are not a citizen of any other country.
  2. You are not enrolled in any other constituency.
  3. You understand false declaration is punishable under law.
Missing any box = automatic rejection.

Step 6: Signature or Left Thumb Impression

Use blue ink. Women and elderly should use left thumb impression. Men can sign. If you are absent, any adult family member can sign on your behalf. BLO will countersign below.

Step 7: Submit to BLO During Home Visit

Do not mail or submit online. Wait for BLO visit (Nov 2025 – Jan 2026). Hand over printed form with documents. Get acknowledgment slip. If missed, visit your polling station with form.
